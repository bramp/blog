Alternative Milks

2021-04-03 | milk

I’ve not been getting out as much during Covid, as such I’ve tried to reduce my caloric intake. One way I tried to do this, was to switch the kind of milk I drink. This also had a secondary impact on reducing my environment impact. However, while researching the various milks, I couldn’t find one source that put both nutritional information, and environment impact of the various milks in one place. This article does just that.
